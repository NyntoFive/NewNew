Monoblock Gear Clip™ - (1.50 Inch) - (w/Spartan Helmet Engraving) - (w/Mounting Hardware)
Stock# DCC-MONOBLK-15-WH-1-SPARTAN QTY DISCOUNTS (See Below)

Price: $8.99


The DCC Monoblock belt clip is designed to be mounted directly to a holster/sheath surface. This clip design allows for a very secure carry on belts, waistlines, or any attachment point where the spring steel clip tabs can be positioned securely. Each spring tab has an integrated security clutch to prevent it from easily becoming detached from the mounting point. The Monoblock clip comes in a standard DMG/Cu finish in dark matte grey and is non-reflective. It resists corrosion very well and can be buffed with most over-the-counter lubricants to remove surface blemishes and wear marks. This clip is a versatile and cost effective attachment solution that can be adapted to virtually any gun holster, knife sheath or carry item. This model is Dark Gray (DMG/Cu) and features a Spartan Helmet Engraving on the front of the belt clip.

This version includes mounting hardware. (See below for specifications)

Specifications
Overall Length: 2.73 inches
Fits: Up to 1.50 inch Belts
Finish: Dark Grey (DMG/Cu)
Engraving: Spartan Helmet
Reflective: No
Hole Pattern: (2)(Slotted) (0.220 height x 0.300 width) inches
Hole Spacing: (Adjustable) (0.860 - 1.15 on centers) inches
Hardware Included: Yes (2) Mounting Screws , (2) Slotted Posts , (2) Washers
